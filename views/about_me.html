About me


Hi there! I'm Jacob Hough! I'm currently studying computer science at the University of Colorado in Boulder. I'm highly interested in system security and software development. I think the future is in technology and I love that I get to be a part of the evolving generation of tech. A few interesting things about me: I was born and raised here in Colorado (specifically Fort Lupton and Longmont) and I decided to not go too far and that wound me up here in Boulder! I love photography and music. I've played trumpet for the better part of 9 years now and in those 9 years, I've participated in all sorts of ensembles ranging from jazz bands, concert or classical bands, to marching bands. I'm still playing on my own time and in the CU marching band! I graduated from Longmont High School in 2017 and thus began my journey. So far I've taken plenty of CS classes that have vastly expanded my knowledge on just how powerful machines today have gotten. Some of those classes include Intro to Computing, Data Structures, Systems, and Software Development Methods and Tools. Those are my cores, but I've learned plenty of applicable things along the way!
